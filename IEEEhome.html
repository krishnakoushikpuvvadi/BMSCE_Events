The Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an organization aimed at inculcating the spirit of wisdom and knowledge. BMSCE IEEE Student Branch was founded in 2009 under the guidance of Dr. Ravishankar Deekshit with Ashwith Jerome Rego as the founding chairperson. With events and workshops conducted throughout the year, we make sure our members are kept up to date with the evolving needs of our technological world. Over the course of 12 years, we have grown immensely, and now, we are the 7th largest Student Branch in the world. With such achievements, we have established ourselves as the technical society to be in BMSCE.
